About WolverineCS

WolverineCS is a student made organization at Clear Brook High School. This organization was made in the hopes to inspire, gather and create a group of talented individuals interested in computer science. The full mission plan is to bring people who want to engage themselves in programming into a world of opportunities, and connect them in such a way that it prepares them for the future.

What We Hope to Accomplish

WolverineCS's overall goal is to create an environment afterschool for kids who would like to share their passion for computer science. By creating a community such as this for the students, we will be able to help them decide if computer science is the right fit for them in college and hopefully teach them a thing or two about programming.
